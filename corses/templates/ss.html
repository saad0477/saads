About Me

Welcome to my website! I'm SAAD, a recent graduate of the National Higher School of Computer Science and Systems Analysis (ENSIAS) in Rabat, where I obtained my MASTER degree in Data Science. My academic background has also enabled me to develop my web development skills. I have a solid grounding in the creation of interactive and responsive websites, which enables me to combine the power of data science with the user-friendliness of web development to create innovative solutions.
